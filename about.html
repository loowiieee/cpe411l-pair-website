About HABI-LIN

Preserving Culture Through Technology

Our Mission

HABI-LIN (Learning, Integration, and Navigation) is dedicated to bridging the gap between traditional weaving techniques and modern digital tools.


What we offer:

  • Digital Archiving: Saving traditional patterns for future generations.
  • 3D Visualization: Helping weavers see patterns before the first thread is laid.
  • Hardware Integration: Connecting looms to digital interfaces.
